Our Techniques

We use cutting-edge methods from AI safety research to decode how foundation models understand biology.

Sparse Autoencoders

We train specialized neural networks to "disentangle" the messy internal representations of foundation models. This converts overlapping signals into a clean dictionary of monosemantic features, where each feature corresponds to a specific biological concept like a cell type, pathway, or disease state.

Circuit Tracing

Once we have the feature dictionary, we map the connections between features to trace the model's "thought process." This reveals the exact causal circuits the model uses to predict how a drug affects a cell, exposing the mechanism of action.

Biological Validation

We validate discovered circuits against established biological databases like Gene Ontology, KEGG, and Reactome. This lets us distinguish genuine biological insights from statistical noise, and identify novel mechanisms for experimental validation.
